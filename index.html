About us

Vinyl Record Store is a store specializing in collectible records and vinyls of all formats and focused mainly on the 60s to 90s. We have special sections of records.

Spanish and British with the rarest records you can imagine as well as a system of disk search on request. Also, music books, comics, and movie covers.

Our philosophy is to recover forgotten records and take care of them as part of the audiovisual culture.

We travel the planet to find that album you are looking for.
