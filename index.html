About Us

Vinyl Record Store specializes in collectible records and vinyls from the 60s to the 90s. We have special sections for rare Spanish and British records.

We also offer a record search system, as well as music books, comics, and movie covers. Our philosophy is to recover forgotten records and preserve them as part of audiovisual culture.

We travel the planet to find that album you are looking for.
